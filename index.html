Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzimaki in Naruto Shippuden. Hover the mouse to see the villain, Orochimaru.

Early Childhood

Naruto Uzimaki was an outcast. Everybody treated him differently, and that made him a troublemaker. He goofed around alot and was considered an outcast. There was a beast that destroyed the whole village that was sealed in him by the Fourth Hokage. This gives him special abilities like healing himself quicker than others. The beast is not what makes him strong though, his desire to become hokage, is what made him strong. He was the weakest when he was young, failed all the tests, and was the class clown. Now he's up to par with all the other ninjas because he worked hard for it.

Team Seven

Everybody was split into teams according to their grades. You had Ino, a girl who has a crush on Sasuke, Shikomaru, smartest character and plays ninja like it's chess, and chogi, a character that get made fun of because of his weight but is really strong, led by Asuma Sarutobi; then, you got Rock Lee, a guy that doesn't use his chakra, but is very fast and uses his Taijutsu, Neji, who can attack people chakra points, and Tenten, led by Might Guy; Then you got Hinata, who is very shy, and has a crush on Naruto, Kiba, who travels with a dog companion, and Shino who fights with bugs, led by Kurenai Yuhi; and then you got Naruto, his rival/best friend Sasuke, and Sakura, another girl who also has a crush on Sasuke. Those three were led by Kakashi, the copycat ninja. Kakashi challenged them to get the bells hanging out of his pocket, and they all tried their best, but failed. It was so easy, that Kakashi challenged them while reading a book! The rule was that they had to do it before eating lunch or they will get tied up. Naruto later got tied up and was forbidden to eat food while the others were eating. Sasuke and Sakura felt bad and both of them offered a portion of their food. Kakashi came out of nowhere and told them surprisingly that they past for teamwork! Every shinobi should work as a team, and so their long journey begins.

Challenges he faced

He had some rivals, and hardships he faced. Orochimaru was the main antagonist in his Childhood. He's the only immortal villain and his ultimate goal was to learn every Jutsu! He later assassinated the third hokage, but the hokage in return, sealed his hands, so he can't perform any hand jutsu! That doesn't stop Orochimaru from getting what he wants though. He later cursed Sasuke Uchiha knowing that he will want to reach out to him to get more power to kill his older brother, Itachi Uchiha! Then Orochimaru will try to use his body for himself! Naruto also faced another rival that was like him, that also had a beast sealed in him, Gaara. Gaara was the antagonist in Naruto early days, but he later changed and achieved his purpose of becoming Kazekage of the Sand village. Naruto also felt loneliness, Sakura thought Naruto was a nuisance, and Sasuke was always better and stronger than him. This just made Naruto keep pushing himself, and later, everybody started to believe in him!

Naruto vs Sasuke

The ultimate battle begins! Sasuke used Naruto as a best friend to later try to kill him! Sasuke followed his brother footsteps. They got a special power called the Sharingan which allows you to see what the human eye can't see. To unlock it's full potential, you must kill your loved one. When Naruto and Sasuke battled, Sasuke nearly kill Naruto, but decided not to. He wanted to try to find another way to achieve his power so he went on to train with Orochimaru.
